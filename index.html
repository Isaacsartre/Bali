OVERVIEW

The St. Regis Bali Resort ... invitingly tranquil and elegant, this resort's peaceful repose suggests a barefoot stroll on soft, sandy beaches, or a dive into the turquoise lagoons. Situated next door to the Bali National Golf Club, The St. Regis Bali Resort has been placed upon the wistful shores of Nusa Dua, the Garden of Bali. A lavishly appointed private residence on the beach, all of this five star resort's 123 luxurious suites and villas are pure magic, filled with handcrafted Balinese art, blissful balconies, and marble bathrooms. King-sized beds and splashes of vibrant Balinese colors will transport you to a different time, when life was always new, exciting, and extraordinary. Your culinary options are limitless here, and a unique rendezvous like a gourmet picnic on the beach is always a good idea at The St. Regis Bali Resort. Try to resist flinging yourself into that spectacular sea mid-meal, or your dining partner might be a bit miffed. In the tradition of St Regis hotels, the Remede Spa is the perfect place to be pampered, and there should always be time for a round or two of golf at the Golf & Country Club. Kids will be entertained in a stimulating manner at the Children's Learning Center, while a spectacular Salt Water Lagoon is perfect for splashing about in.

Location

The St. Regis Bali Resort is ideally located on the southern tip of Bali, in the centre of the Indonesian archipelago. The resort is a 15-minute cab-ride away and easily accessible from the Denpasar Airport (also known as Ngurah Rai International). The Bali area is known for its beaches and quaint local villages. Pacifica Art Museum, Seminyak, Ubud Art Village, Besakih Temple, Lovina are all within reach.
